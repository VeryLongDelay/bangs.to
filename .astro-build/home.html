Local-first Private by design

Fast search routing that feels reliable.

ban.gs lets your browser turn shortcuts like !gh react or !w saturn into the right destination without shipping every query through a remote redirect page.

Trust signal

Runs on your device

Service Worker routing keeps the redirect logic in the browser, not on someone else’s server.

Setup

Speed

Zero extra page loads

Why it feels fast

No extra redirect page in the middle.

ban.gs resolves the route before a normal page load has to happen, so the interaction feels closer to a built-in browser feature than a web app detour.
